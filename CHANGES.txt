KEY IMPROVEMENTS
================

COLORS
------
Backgrounds:  zinc-950, zinc-900

Label: text-xs font-semibold uppercase tracking-wide
Body: text-sm font-normal

COMPONENTS
----------
Card:    rounded-lg border-zinc-800/50 shadow-sm hover:shadow-md
Badge:   rounded-md px-2 py-1 border /10 bg + /20 border
Button:  rounded-lg active:scale-95 transition-all duration-200
Input:   rounded-lg border-zinc-700/50 bg-zinc-900/50 focus:ring-primary/30
Modal:   rounded-xl border-zinc-800/50 bg-zinc-900 shadow-2xl

TRANSITIONS
-----------
Standard:   duration-200 (hover, color changes)
Modal:      duration-300 (animations)
Progress:   duration-500 (visual effect)
Transform:  active:scale-95 (feedback)

RESPONSIVENESS
--------------

Desktop: > 1024px  - Grid 4+ cols, p-8, all visible, icons h-5 w-5
